Sample Projects:

SenyasFSL is a gamified Filipino Sign Language (FSL) learning app designed to make learning sign language more engaging, interactive, and accessible. It uses gesture recognition technology powered by MediaPipe and TensorFlow to track hand movements and recognize signs in real time, allowing users to practice and receive feedback instantly. The app transforms learning into a fun experience through gameplay elements such as challenges, progress tracking, and rewards, encouraging consistent practice. SenyasFSL aims to bridge communication gaps, promote inclusivity, and empower learners—whether beginners, educators, or the Deaf community—with a modern and interactive tool for mastering Filipino Sign Language.
